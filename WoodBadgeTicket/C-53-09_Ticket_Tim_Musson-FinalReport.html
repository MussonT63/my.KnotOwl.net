  1. Done 2010.02.18 ! -- Draw up a Crossover Ceremony

  3. Done 2010.05.14 ! -- Create a teaching knot board including virtual how to's & Become a Pioneering MBC
    • Who: Scouts and leaders learning and teaching Scoutting knots
    • When: December 2010
    Fall 2009, I became a Pioneering MBC. I created some teaching knot boards for the Chief Okemos Council's 100 Year Camporee where I ran a knot station called "I'm all tied up". It was a lot of fun sharing my enjoyment of knots and ropes... Everyone that stopped by had fun (and learned something new!).

    I really didn't like the idea of a knot board, so I expanded on it to create a "Knot Masters" program that is the basis of the KnotOwl.net web site. I have the start of the Knot Masters program online, and will be adding things over the next couple of years until I like the final product (which means I will be making changes forever, LOL). Knot Masters is a program where Scouts and Scouters learn various knots and other things to do with rope. As you learn more and more complex knots, you move up through different colored Para Cords.

    1. Apprentice=White
    2. Knotter=Yellow
    3. Hitcher=Red
    4. Master=Blue
    5. Owl=Green
    6. Lasher=Overhand in ends
    7. Maker=Overhand in bight
