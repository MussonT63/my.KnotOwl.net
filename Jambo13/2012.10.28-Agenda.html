Jamboree Meeting 2012/10/28

  • By 10/1/2013, everyone should have paid at least $900.
  • Photos must be current in the Summit profile by 3/31/2013. They start producing IDs then.

Information distilled from https://summit.scouting.org/en/Jamboree2013/Pages/Council.aspx#webinars

  • There will be specific Medical forms for Jambo.
  • Medical forms will be sent via eMail.
  • Visitors are strongly encouraged to register online. There will be a limited number allowed each day and if you don't register, you aren't guarenteed to get in.
  • The current word on duffel bags: 2'x2'x3', 115 liters and out 2nd quarter 2013.
  • Units bring their own flag, pole is provided. US flage and pole provided.
  • White Watter Rafting is an added fee of $50, paid when you register for the activity.
  • In November the list of activities that need to be signed up for should be available.
  • The activities that need registration should have the registration available late Dec / early Jan.
  • Activity signups are first come/first served.
  • The "Gateway" is a 1' x 8' banner.
  • Camp site is about 36' x 180' for all sites.
  • Jamboree Catalog should be out first quarter 2013
  • Arrival/Departure times are coordinated through TMS.
  • Weather at the "shakedown" was high 80's with mild humidity.
  • "Official Venturing uniform is the green shirt and gray pants/shorts."
  • Venture Crew Contingent unit number is 510 C or D
  • Venture contingents need to coordinate with other contingents in the campsite to decide "Unit" level things
    • Flag
    • Banner
    • President, etc
